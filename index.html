Our Story

Established in 2026, "Parking Hub" began with a simple vision: to make urban navigation and parking accessible to every Filipino driver. What started as a small project to solve city congestion has grown into a trusted platform, serving thousands of commuters daily.

We work directly with commercial establishment owners and private lot operators to ensure that every parking space meets our strict safety and accessibility standards. Our commitment to innovation and driver satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for daily commuters and travelers across the region.

Frequently asked questions

If you can't find what you are looking for don't hesitate to contact us.

What is Parking Hub?
Parking Hub is a smart parking management system designed to help drivers find and reserve available parking spaces in real-time within the city.
How does Parking Hub help me find parking?
Our platform provides a real-time map showing available slots, pricing, and exact locations of parking lots nearby.
What kind of parking information is available?
We provide real-time slot availability, pricing details, operating hours, and specific amenities (like CCTV, 24/7 security, or covered parking).
Can I search for specific types of parking?
Yes, you can use our filter tools to search by vehicle type (2-wheels or 4-wheels) and preferred amenities like handicap accessibility or gated facilities.
Is it free to use?
Browsing for parking locations is free. Making a reservation may involve a small booking fee depending on the parking lot operator.
How accurate is the information?
Our data is synced directly with smart sensors and parking gate systems to ensure real-time accuracy of available slots.
How can I report outdated or incorrect parking information?
You can report discrepancies via the "Report Issue" button on the parking lot profile page or by contacting our support team directly.
What devices is Parking Hub available on?
Parking Hub is fully responsive and accessible via any web browser on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.
What are the future plans for Parking Hub?
We are working on integrating EV charging station maps, monthly subscription plans, and AI-powered traffic prediction models to help you plan your parking even better.
